关于我们About Us

上海润安医学科技有限公司 Shanghai Runan Medical Technology Ltd., Co.

上海润安医学科技有限公司是一家专注于基因合成与分子生物学服务的国家高新技术企业。公司位于上海张江科学城，依托资深科研团队和先进的合成生物学平台，为生命科学研究、药物开发和生物制造提供高质量的基因相关服务。

我们的核心团队由来自中科院、复旦大学、上海交通大学等知名院所的专业人才组成，在基因工程、分子克隆、蛋白质表达等领域拥有深厚的技术积累和丰富的项目经验。

Shanghai Runan Medical Technology Ltd., Co. is a national high-tech enterprise specializing in gene synthesis and molecular biology services. Based in Shanghai Zhangjiang Science City, our experienced team and advanced synthetic biology platform deliver high-quality gene-related services for life science research, drug development, and biomanufacturing.

Our core team consists of professionals from top institutions including CAS, Fudan University, and Shanghai Jiao Tong University, bringing deep technical expertise and extensive project experience in genetic engineering, molecular cloning, and protein expression.
